3D Scene Creation
Creating a 3D scene involves combining art, technology, and storytelling. Using software like Blender or 3ds Max, we craft detailed, lifelike models of objects, characters, and environments. Lighting Mastery

Materials and Textures
Our texturing process involves meticulous UV mapping and texture painting using tools like Substance Painter and Photoshop. Rendering and Animation
Using top-tier rendering engines, we produce high-resolution images and animations. We also optimize scenes for real-time applications, ensuring clean performance without compromising visual fidelity.
Post-Processing
The post-processing phase involves composing rendered layers, adding special effects, and refining color grading with software like After Effects and more. The result is a visual symphony that transcends the ordinary, captivating hearts and minds alike.

Keymavens Headquarters

AR/VR
Research and Project Study
After thoroughly analyzing the project, including its dimensions and target audience, we delve into every detail to fully understand the vision and requirements. Our experts conduct in-depth research to ensure the final product perfectly aligns with your expectations.
Design Creation
By taking preferences into account, we develop a unique design concept. This process involves creating mood boards and defining the stylistic direction, helping visualize the overall idea and atmosphere of the project. We meticulously refine every detail to ensure the design is cohesive and aesthetically pleasing.
3D Scene Assembly
Once the design concept is approved, the next step is creating a detailed 3D scene. We model all elements of the project with high precision and detail. For exterior projects, we also develop a landscape mood board to provide a comprehensive view of the surroundings.
Cleaning and Optimization
For VR projects, we perform a thorough cleaning and optimization of the 3D model. This ensures high performance and smooth operation in virtual reality.
Transfer to Unreal Engine (VR only)
We transferred the project to Unreal Engine, leveraging its powerful capabilities for VR development, including high-quality graphics and interactivity.
Lighting Setup and Gray Frames (GOM - grey override material)
At this stage, we set up lighting and create gray frames for preliminary review. This allows us to check the composition and overall atmosphere of the project before adding materials and textures.
Material and Texture Work
We carefully develop all materials and textures to make the project as realistic as possible. By creating and fine-tuning surfaces, adding details, and incorporating effects, we achieve a high level of realism.
Blueprint Development (VR only)
In VR projects, we develop interactive functionality using blueprints. This allows users to change models and materials in real-time and interact with the project, creating a unique user experience.
Baking (AR only)
The final stage involves baking all data into a USDZ file, providing easy use and compatibility with various platforms, and facilitating further work on the project.
We aim to exceed our clients' expectations by providing them with state-of-the-art AR and VR solutions. Trust our expertise and professionalism, and watch your project come to life with vivid new dimensions!
