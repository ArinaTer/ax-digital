Naming
Naming is all about considering all aspects of a brand's existence when coming up with a name. We think about how it would sound when spoken, how it would be written in news releases, what it would look like, and how it would adapt to social media and mobile apps.
Design Strategy
Design strategies treat design as a building block (using references) and provide various combinations of elements based on different areas of brand positioning while also determining the most promising direction together with the customer.
Visual Identity
Corporate identity is a system of identification and the visual language of the brand. We create it, based on brand positioning, taking into account the specifics of user engagement points. When developing corporate identity, we think about where the brand will live, and we build a flexible but understandable system that will work effectively in any case.
Infographics and Animation
Infographics allow you to convey information in a simple and clear way. With it, we can briefly and easily outline a large volume of complex text or visualize a process. Infographics provide maximum information with minimal user effort.
Brand Book
The final stage of the development of the brand identity is the regulation of the rules of its application. We draw up several documents, each of which covers a certain range of tasks. At the same time, all of them are aimed at making work with the brand simple, understandable, and convenient, both for marketers, brand managers, and designers in-house and for external partners.
Brand Audit
A brand audit is a diagnostic of its key parameters and characteristics for understanding further ways of development. First of all, it answers the question of what stays and what changes in rebranding.
